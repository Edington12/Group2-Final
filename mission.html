Our Mission

Inspiring, Empowering and Engaging the Next Generation of Students

Our mission is to open a world of possibility for all children while making a difference in every child’s life. This is done by providing school supplies along with all basic needs to children so they can thrive in school. Our job is to take the worry out of every child’s school day by providing books, supplies and necessities that they may need. This allows children to focus on learning and continuing to grow so they can reach their full potential. Our hope is that this will impact not only this generation but many future generations to come. This can only be achieved through empowerment, caring, respect, team work, high standards and accessibility for all. We love helping children one book at a time and that is why we built this website, concept and continue to build partnerships within all communities and the local parent/student associations in order to accomplish everything we set out to do. This includes; but, is not limited to building community partnerships with the local foodbanks along with many other organizations within the community. Our work helps students, their families, teachers within the community.
